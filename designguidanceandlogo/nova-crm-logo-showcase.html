Usage Guidelines

✅ Do

  • Use on dark backgrounds (#1e1e2e or darker)
  • Maintain minimum clear space of 20px around logo
  • Scale proportionally when resizing
  • Use icon version for sizes below 100px width
  • Keep the orange accent as the primary brand color

❌ Don't

  • Don't rotate or distort the logo
  • Don't change the colors without approval
  • Don't add effects like drop shadows or outlines
  • Don't use on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Don't recreate or redraw the logo

Background Examples

ℹ️ The logo is optimized for dark backgrounds (Catppuccin Mocha theme). For light backgrounds, a separate variant would be needed with adjusted colors.

Design Concept

The Nova CRM logo embodies the concept of explosive growth and transformation. A nova is a stellar explosion that dramatically increases a star's brightness — symbolizing how Nova CRM illuminates customer relationships and drives business growth.


The radiating star design uses the Innovaas Orange (#F25C05) as the core, with softer orange rays and blue accent particles representing data points and connections. The geometric, modern aesthetic aligns with the Catppuccin Mocha color scheme, creating a cohesive brand identity that feels both professional and innovative.
